How to Request Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your EcoCheck account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

  1. Send an email to ecocheckapp@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include the email address registered to your EcoCheck account
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days
Send Deletion Request

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Full name (first, middle, last)
  • Email address
  • Contact number
  • Account password (hashed)
  • Submitted reports and associated photos
  • Points and activity history

Data Retention

Upon deletion, all personal data is permanently removed from our systems immediately. Anonymous, non-identifiable report data (location of environmental issue, status) may be retained for up to 30 days for environmental record-keeping purposes, after which it is also permanently deleted.

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, your account and data cannot be recovered.
